🏦 Your Safety Piggy Banks!
Smart investors save money each month for three important things:
💡 These are like safety nets! By saving a little each month, you're prepared for anything!
🚪
Vacancy Fund
Sometimes tenants move out and it takes time to find new ones. During that time, the house is empty (vacant) and you're not collecting rent!
$170/mo
Setting aside 10% of your rent means you'll have money saved up to cover the mortgage even when the house is empty. Most investors budget for the house being empty about 1 month per year.
🔧
Repairs Fund
Things break! The toilet might leak, the dishwasher could stop working, or the fence might need fixing. These are small repairs that happen regularly.
$170/mo
Saving 10% of your rent each month means when something breaks, you have the money ready to fix it quickly! Happy tenants = steady rent checks!
🏗️
Big Stuff Fund (CapEx)
Every 10-20 years, BIG things need replacing: the roof, the furnace, the water heater, or the appliances. These are expensive!
$170/mo
By saving 10% each month, when the roof needs replacing in 15 years (about $10,000!), you'll have $18,000+ saved up. No panic, no problem!
